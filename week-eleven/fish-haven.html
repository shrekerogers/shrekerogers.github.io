Local Hobbies and Veterans

By: Monica Stevens

Recently we sat down with a local Army Veteran to see what he does for therapy after life in the military and now having PTSD. YOu would ask or think how does this relate to the weather? The answer is it does. The local veterans Name is John Atwood and he uses the hobby of R.C. "remote control" vehicles both on the ground and the air for his self-help therapy. This hobby is impacted by the weather going on outside. John explained that knowing what the weather is for him is crucial before leaving the house to determine what he will be flying or driving on the ground. It also can help him determine if he will be going out to drive or fly or staying home to work on his R.C. vehicles if the weather is bad. Recently John has connected through social media to others in the R.C. community. He stated that someday he wants to start an R.C. non-profit group for veterans that supplies them with the nessecary skils, like laws and rules, and their first R.C. vehicle and everything needed to get out driving or flying. John we hope someday you can accomplish this!
