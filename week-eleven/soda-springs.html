Freaky Weather

By: Jamie Smith

This years weather has been some what out of the ordinary. In years past we had plenty of snow and cold to match. This year though snow has been in the mountians we have had a slow snow year in Soda Springs. This begs the question will we have water in the summer and will it be a hot summer? Only time will tell. Three years age was a crazy time with record anounts of snow throught out the whole state of Idaho. What will the year ahead bring us? Only time will tell but is there anything wrong with a not as cold winter and warmer summer? I for one would enjoy the possibilty of a warmer summer and more mild winter compared to three years ago. What are everybodies thoughts on the future weather this coming summer?
