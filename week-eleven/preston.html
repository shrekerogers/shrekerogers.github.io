After Napleon Dynamite

By: Janica Juarez

It has been a over decade since the movie Napleon Dynmaite was filmed her in PReston locally. It was exciting to have Holywood at our doorsteps and the potential to shed a good light on our tiny town in Idaho. The possiblities seem endless right because of the spotlight? Ten years and beyond not much has changed for Preston and it remains basically the same quite town in Southern Idaho. Not much has changed and we still go about our normal lives as if nothing happened here. So what happend to the possibilites or is this town like the typical small town Amercia that gets glimpses of the outside world that never stay? The prospect of small town is appealing to most locals and most would say they are glad the town has not changed much. It stayed a quite and affordable place to live!
