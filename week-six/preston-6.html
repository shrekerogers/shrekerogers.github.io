Founders Day Fresco

In repsonse, I contacted Ms. Sharon via email and expressed my desire to serve on the event committee. Well, you guessed it, the phone rings almost immediately and she promptly ask me to be the event committee chair. Literally, good grief! My apprication for the years of dedicated, generous service of the volunteers and the sponsors has escalted beyond what I thought I could feel....
